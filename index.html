Louis Braille and The Evolution of Braille

Louis Braille was a French educator and inventor who developed the system of reading and writing for the visually impaired known as Braille.

Braille Reading and Writing process

Braille Reading and Writing Process course provides a comprehensive understanding of the Braille system, focusing on the skills and techniques necessary for reading and writing using Braille

The Techniques of Teaching Braille course is designed to provide educators, caregivers, and professionals working with individuals who are blind or visually impaired the necessary skills and strategies to effectively teach Braille

Frequently Asked Questions

How do i know the right courses for me?

You can identify the right course by a combination of self-reflection,research and exploration and also by identifying your interests,accessing your skills&strengths,seek advice and trust your instincts

How to proceed further after selecting the course?

After selecting the course there are some considerations like contacting the disablity service office, utilize assistive technology,communicate with instructors,opt for note-taking support and connect with peers

How does this website works?

This website helps to register for volunteers and gives informations about courses and contacts.The volunteers can convey or explain to the students and can get their opinions at the time of service which will help us to develop in future.

How does these courses help the students?

In current situation there is a competition for everything especially for job and career.By studying these courses the students get personal growth and fulfillment,knowledge and skill development,qualifications,credentials and also career advancement

Will the students get certification after completing the course

It is important to research and review the details of the course you are considering to determine if certification is available and if it aligns with your goals and aspirations. Look for information on the course website or reach out to the institution

Which is the first and basic course to be learned?

Like alphabets(A-Z) and numbers(0-9) visually impaired students must learn the basics of braille and must proceed further in that,Without learning braille many students are dependent on Audio materials and reading sessions.

Can the students request a note-taker or assistance with taking notes in Braille?

Yes,as a student,you can typically request a note-taker or assistance with taking notes in Braille. Many educational institutions have support services or disability services offices that can provide accommodations for students including assistance

Can the student receive Braille versions of exams or assessments?

Yes, as a student, you can typically request Braille versions of exams or assessments. Educational institutions are typically committed to providing equal access to all students

How can I access course materials in Braille?

The students can access the course materials through transcription services,online respiratories and libraries, braille embossers(they convert into braille) and by contacting the institution & instructors.
